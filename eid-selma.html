✦   A Message from the Heart   ✦

There are feelings that words can only barely hold —
and yet here I am, trying.

Selma, this Eid carries a meaning it never had before. Because now I celebrate it knowing you are in my life — and that alone makes this the most blessed day of the year. You are not just someone I love. You are the calm inside the chaos, the warmth I return to, the person whose laugh I want to hear first thing in the morning and whose name I whisper when I am grateful.

I am grateful — deeply, sincerely grateful — for every moment you have chosen to let me in. Every conversation, every silence shared, every time you looked at me and I forgot what I was about to say. You have made ordinary days feel like gifts. And I do not want to take a single one for granted.

I want to spend the rest of my life making sure you know how rare you are. Not in grand gestures and empty promises — but in presence, in patience, in choosing you again and again, every single day.

✦   I wrote you something   ✦

To Selma, with everything I have

My love, I have been searching for the right words since the first moment I realised that what I feel for you is not something that fits neatly inside sentences. But tonight, on this sacred night of Eid, I want to try.

You walked into my life and everything quietly rearranged itself. Not dramatically — not in the way they write in novels. Quietly. The way light changes in a room when you pull back the curtain. Suddenly things looked different. Warmer. More possible.

I love the way your mind works — curious and honest and beautifully yours. I love that you push me to be better without ever making me feel small. I love that with you I can be still, and that stillness feels like enough.

On this Eid, I am not just wishing you happiness. I am promising to be part of the reason you have it. I want to be the person who stands beside you — through every celebration and every hard day in between.

So here it is, simply: I love you, Selma. Not as an emotion that comes and goes — but as a decision I make every morning when I wake up. A quiet, steady, certain choice. Forever.

— Yours, always ✦
